QR Quest Hints Generator!

Procedure:

  • Enter the hint in the given Text Box.
  • Choose the Group code for which you want to generate the hint.
  • Select the Hint number that you want to generate.
  • Finally hit that Generate button.
  • Right Click on the QR Code that is generated and click on "Save image as" to save that hint.
  • Congratulate yourself on "Going Paperless" :)
